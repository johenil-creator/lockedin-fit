Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

LockedIn FIT ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by Johenil Hernandez. This policy describes how the app handles your information.

Data Storage

LockedIn FIT is a local-first app. All workout data, plans, progress, and preferences are stored on your device using local storage. Your data is not sent to external servers unless you explicitly choose to use optional cloud features.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

The app can optionally read and write data from Apple Health, including:

  • Body weight
  • Workouts
  • Heart rate and resting heart rate
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Step count
  • Active energy burned
  • Sleep analysis

This data is used solely to provide readiness scoring, recovery insights, and workout tracking within the app. Health data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers, sold, or shared with third parties.

Account & Authentication

The app offers optional sign-in via Google. If you choose to sign in, your email address and display name are used for account identification and optional cloud backup via Firebase. We do not share this information with third parties.

Data Collection

We do not collect, track, or sell your personal data. The app does not include any analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking.

Third-Party Services

If you use optional cloud features, the app uses Google Firebase for authentication and data storage. Firebase's privacy policy applies to data processed through their services.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
